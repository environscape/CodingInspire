VFAM使用操作流程:

1.将外部时钟信号连接至VFAM的ext adv in接口

2.将VFAM的adv out连接至DFAM的adv/clock接口

3.按下DFAM的advace按钮切换到它认定的第一步

并按下DFAM的run/stop按钮使它处于随时启动的状态

4.按下VFAM的run/stop按钮正式开始驱动DFAM步进

5.按下VFAM的stop或rst按钮将会止步进,

并通过adv out迅速触发剩余步进使DFAM回到第一步

在音序播放的状态中 您也可以随时按下set 1按钮,

实时指定您认为的第一步

### VFAM Operating Procedure:

Connect the external clock signal to the VFAM's ext adv in port.

Connect the VFAM's ADV OUT port to the DFAM's ADV/CLOCK port.

Press the DFAM's advance button to switch to its designated first step

and press the DFAM's run/stop button to keep it ready to start at any time.

Press the VFAM's run/stop button to officially start driving the DFAM step sequence.

Pressing the VFAM's stop or rst button will stop the stepping, and through the adv out,

quickly trigger the remaining steps to return the DFAM to the first step.

In the state of playing the sequence, you can also press the set 1 button at any time 

to specify in real-time what you consider to be the first step.
